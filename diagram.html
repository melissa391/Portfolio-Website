About

I am a digital designer, trained in UX/UI, theories of cognition and agile design methodologies. Through freelance graphic design projects and my MSc in Human-Computer Interaction Design, I have developed creative problem-solving skills and the ability to work efficiently to client briefs. My BA in Graphic Design from Camberwell College of Arts taught me typography and layout skills. I am also experienced in web development (HTML5, CSS and JavaScript) and prototyping software (Sketch, Axure RP & Adobe XD).

In my freetime, I enjoy going to art exhibitions and live music events, cooking and playing football.
