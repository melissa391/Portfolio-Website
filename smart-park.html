Smart Park Research Project

The Problem

Working with a team to explore opportunities for smart technologies in public parks. Interviews and observation in a number of London parks brought up several issues, such as:

  • Lack of community
  • Broken equipment/ park furniture
  • Lack of facilities
  • Use of park and certain facilities is weather permitting
  • Safety

Next Steps

  • Personas were created based on several dozen interviews with people in Greenwich Park.
  • We ideated with crazy eights and brainstorming techniques in order to generate meaningful design solutions.
  • A tool kit was produced, which allows the community to co-design their parks.
